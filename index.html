I cover the hidden underbelly of the digital world—how tech companies manipulate you (and each other) in their fight to dominate the internet.

Today I'm a Senior Technology Journalist at the BBC and a leading voice for tech news on TikTok. I’ve spent nearly a decade exposing the AI, surveillance systems, and online power struggles that distort our lives, along with the regulatory failures that let it all happen.

My work has sparked U.S. Senate hearings, triggered FTC lawsuits, and changed federal law. After my investigation into Meta's privacy practices, the company deleted facial recognition data for over a billion people.

I also regularly appear on outlets including CNN, NPR, the Today Show, and CBS Mornings, despite the algorithms' best efforts.
